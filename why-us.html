Why Mwiwa

There are several borehole drilling companies operating in Zimbabwe. Here is exactly what sets Mwiwa apart — and why it matters to your project.

The Difference

Four Reasons to Choose Mwiwa

Every point below has a direct, practical impact on your project's cost, speed, and outcome.

01

We Own Our Equipment

Most drilling companies in Zimbabwe are brokers. They take your enquiry, hire a rig from a third party, and manage a subcontractor you've never met. That means delays when the rig is elsewhere, inflated quotes to cover middlemen margins, and no direct accountability when something goes wrong.

Mwiwa is different. We own and operate our own modern heavy drilling rig. It is maintained by us, scheduled by us, and arrives on your site with our own crew. When we commit to a start date, we can honour it — because we control the machinery.

No third-party rig hire delays
No subcontractor margin in your quote
Direct accountability on every job
02

Scientific Siting First

The single biggest risk in borehole drilling is drilling in the wrong place. Without a proper geophysical survey, you are essentially guessing where water is beneath the ground. A failed or low-yield borehole wastes your money and leaves you without water.

Before we drill a single metre, we conduct a professional geophysical survey of your site. Our equipment detects subsurface water-bearing fractures and aquifer zones, allowing us to select the drilling point with the highest probability of a productive, high-yield borehole.

Geophysical scanning equipment on every survey
Optimal point selected before any drilling
Significantly reduces risk of low-yield result
03

End-to-End Service

A borehole project involves multiple stages: site survey, drilling, casing and installation, pump supply, pump installation, electrical connections, tank installation, and commissioning. Many companies do one or two of these — leaving you to coordinate the rest yourself.

We handle the complete project from first survey to final handover. Our team carries out every stage in-house — including solar pump systems, elevated tanks, and distribution piping. One team, one timeline, one point of contact. No coordination headaches.

Survey → drilling → installation → pump → handover
Solar systems, tanks, and piping included
Single point of contact throughout
04

Direct & Responsive

When you contact Mwiwa, you reach the team directly. No call centres, no intake forms that go unanswered, no being passed between departments. You get a fast, direct response from people who actually know your project.

WhatsApp is the fastest way to reach us — we respond quickly and can share updates, photos, and documents directly through the chat. For those who prefer a call, our line is direct to the team on-site. Throughout your project, you'll have the same contact person from enquiry to handover.

Same contact person throughout your project
Fast, practical responses — no runaround
