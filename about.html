Who We Are

Harare's Fully Equipped Borehole Company

Mwiwa Borehole Drilling is based in Kamfinsa, Harare, Zimbabwe. We are a fully equipped, professional borehole drilling company — meaning we own and operate our own heavy drilling machinery.

That matters because it means every project is delivered with speed, precision, and direct accountability. No delays waiting for a subcontractor's rig. No inflated costs from third-party equipment hire. Just our team, our rig, and a commitment to getting water to your property.

From the initial geophysical site survey to full pump installation and commissioning, we handle every step in-house. Our experienced team has successfully completed 200+ projects across residential, commercial, agricultural, and industrial sectors throughout Zimbabwe.

How We Work

Our Core Values

Everything we do is guided by a commitment to precision, reliability, and honest service.

Precision

From geophysical siting to final pump calibration, every step is executed with technical accuracy. We don't guess — we measure.

Reliability

Who We Serve

Sectors We Work In

Our team is experienced across four distinct sectors — each with its own water access challenges and requirements.

Residential

Private homes, townhouses, and clusters requiring a reliable, independent water supply.

Commercial

Offices, schools, hospitals, and retail properties that need high-yield, dependable water access.

Agricultural

Farms, irrigation projects, and livestock operations requiring large-volume, off-grid water solutions.

Industrial

Manufacturing, construction, and processing sites with high-demand, continuous water requirements.
