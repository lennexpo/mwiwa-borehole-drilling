About Us

Harare's Water Specialists

Mwiwa Borehole Drilling is a fully equipped, professional borehole drilling company based in Kamfinsa, Harare, Zimbabwe. We own and operate our own heavy drilling machinery, ensuring we deliver every project with speed, precision, and reliability.

From the initial site survey to full pump installation and commissioning, we handle every step in-house. Our experienced team has successfully completed projects across residential, commercial, agricultural, and industrial sectors throughout the region.

Our Services

What We Offer

Complete water access solutions from ground survey to flowing tap — every service delivered with our own equipment and expert crew.

01
🔩
Borehole Drilling

Deep rotary drilling using our modern rig to reach reliable underground aquifers for residential, commercial, and industrial use.

02
🚿
Borehole Installation

Full installation of casing, screens, and headworks to ensure your borehole is properly set up and protected for long-term use.

03
📡
Siting & Surveying

Professional geophysical survey and site siting to identify the best location for maximum water yield before any drilling begins.

04
⚙️
Pump Installation

Supply and installation of submersible and surface pumps, including electrical connections and pump testing for optimal performance.

05
🔄
Borehole Rehabilitation

Restoration of non-functioning or underperforming boreholes through jetting, airlifting, and pump replacement services.

06
🏗️
Water Tank Installation

Supply and installation of above-ground and underground water storage tanks, elevated towers, and distribution piping.

07
☀️
Solar Pump Installation

Off-grid solar-powered pumping systems ideal for farms, remote properties, and areas with unreliable electricity supply.
