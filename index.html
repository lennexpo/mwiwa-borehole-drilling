What We Do

Complete Water Solutions

From the first survey to the final pump test, we handle every stage of your borehole project with our own team and equipment.

Borehole Drilling

Deep rotary drilling using our modern heavy rig to reach reliable underground aquifers, fast and precisely.

Learn more

Borehole Installation

Full installation of casing, screens, and headworks using quality materials built for long-term performance.

Learn more

Siting & Surveying

Geophysical surveys to identify the optimal drilling point — maximising yield before a single metre is drilled.

Learn more

Pump Installation

Submersible and surface pump supply, installation, electrical connections, and full pump testing.

Borehole Rehabilitation

Restoration of non-functioning boreholes through jetting, airlifting, and pump replacement.
