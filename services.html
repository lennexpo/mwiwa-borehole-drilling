Our Services

Seven specialist services — all delivered in-house by our own team and equipment. From site survey to solar pump, we cover your entire project.

What We Offer

Complete Water Solutions

Every service below is delivered directly by Mwiwa — no outsourcing, no subcontracting.

01

Borehole Drilling

We use deep rotary drilling technology with our own modern heavy rig to penetrate the ground and reach reliable underground aquifers. Our drilling team assesses subsurface conditions in real time and adjusts depth and technique accordingly to maximise water yield for your site.

Whether it's a residential property needing 40 metres or an agricultural project requiring 100+ metres, we have the capacity and expertise to complete the job efficiently and precisely.

Rotary Drilling Variable Depth All Ground Types Own Heavy Rig
02

Borehole Installation

Once drilling is complete, we carry out full borehole installation — including casing, screens, gravel pack, and surface headworks. We use quality-rated casing materials appropriate for the ground conditions, ensuring long-term structural integrity and water quality protection.

Proper installation prevents borehole collapse, contamination, and premature pump wear — critical to the lifespan of your borehole investment.

PVC & Steel Casing Screens & Gravel Pack Surface Headworks Quality Materials
03

Siting & Surveying

Before any drilling begins, we conduct a professional geophysical survey to identify the optimal drilling location on your site. This uses subsurface scanning to detect water-bearing fractures and aquifer zones — so we drill where water actually is, not where it looks convenient.

Skipping the survey is the single biggest cause of low-yield or dry boreholes. We never skip it.

Geophysical Survey Site Report Optimal Yield Targeting Risk Reduction
04

Pump Installation

We supply and install submersible and surface pumps to match your property's water demand and borehole yield. Installation includes all electrical connections, rising main, pump control panel, and a full pump test to verify performance before we hand over the system.

We specify the right pump for each application — residential, commercial, and agricultural requirements are all different, and we match the pump accordingly.

Submersible Pumps Surface Pumps Electrical Connections Pump Testing
05

Borehole Rehabilitation

If your existing borehole has reduced yield, stopped flowing, or has a failed pump, we can restore it to full function. Our rehabilitation process includes borehole jetting, airlifting to clear blockages, redevelopment to restore yield, and pump replacement where required.

Rehabilitation is significantly more cost-effective than re-drilling and is often the right first step when an existing borehole underperforms.

Jetting Airlifting Yield Restoration Pump Replacement
06

Water Tank Installation

We supply and install above-ground and underground water storage tanks, elevated water towers, and full distribution piping systems. Proper storage is essential to buffer pump output and ensure a steady supply during peak demand or power outages.

We size and configure tank systems based on your household or operational demand, ensuring sufficient reserve capacity at all times.

Above-Ground Tanks Underground Tanks Elevated Towers Distribution Piping
07

Solar Pump Installation

We design and install off-grid solar-powered pumping systems for farms, remote properties, and locations with unreliable or expensive grid electricity. Solar pump systems eliminate ongoing electricity costs and provide reliable water access even in the most isolated locations.

Each system is sized to match available solar resource, pump demand, and storage — providing a complete, independent water supply solution.

Off-Grid Systems Farm & Agricultural Remote Properties System Sizing
